About MedConnect

A Germany-based career services and education-focused company established in 2019. Specialized as an intermediary in the healthcare sector, we primarily operate in the recruitment of medical professionals, such as doctors and nurses.

With a particular focus on the German market, the company aims to bridge the gap in the healthcare workforce by partnering with hospitals and healthcare institutions. The company has a significant presence in Latin America, specifically in Mexico, where it engages with individuals seeking opportunities in the healthcare industry.

Our Timeline

MedConnect founded

In 2019, MedConnect embarked on its mission to bridge the healthcare gap, establishing itself as a dedicated intermediary connecting Latin American healthcare professionals with opportunities in German hospitals.

2019

MedConnect goes online

Embracing the digital era in 2021, MedConnect launched its online platform, revolutionizing the way healthcare professionals connect with hospitals in Germany, fostering a seamless and accessible recruitment process.

2021

1,000 Members found their job

By the close of 2022, MedConnect celebrated a significant achievement as 1,000 healthcare professionals successfully found rewarding positions through our platform, marking a pivotal moment in empowering careers and addressing workforce needs.

2022

50+ Institutions partnered

In 2022, MedConnect achieved a significant milestone by solidifying agreements with over 50 esteemed healthcare institutions. These strategic alliances not only expanded our network but also fortified our commitment to excellence in healthcare placement.

By fostering strong partnerships with diverse institutions, MedConnect ensured a dynamic and varied array of opportunities for healthcare professionals. This milestone underscores our dedication to creating a robust and interconnected ecosystem that addresses the evolving needs of both healthcare professionals and our partnered institutions.

2022

MedConnect goes global

MedConnect reached new heights in 2023, marking our global expansion. This transformative milestone signifies our commitment to connecting healthcare professionals and institutions beyond borders, fostering a truly international healthcare community.
